A Guide to Enhypen

Enhypen (엔하이픈) is a South Korean boy band formed in 2020 through the survival competition show I-LAND, jointly produced by CJ ENM and Big Hit Entertainment. The group consists of seven members: Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki. Find out more by clicking the tabs above!
